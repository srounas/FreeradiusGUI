Certificates

1. RADIUS server certificate

This is the certificate the RADIUS server presents to clients during EAP-TLS. It should be issued by a CA your Wi-Fi clients already trust (e.g. your internal CA, or the same root you use for Intune Cloud PKI client certs if you want a single trust chain). On save, the certificate and key are parsed and checked that they actually match each other.

2. Trusted CA bundle (client certificate validation)

The CA certificate(s) that issued your Wi-Fi client certificates (e.g. your Intune Cloud PKI root/issuing CA export, or your internal CA chain). Any client certificate signed by one of these CAs will be trusted for EAP-TLS. You can add certificates one at a time or all at once - duplicates are detected automatically.
